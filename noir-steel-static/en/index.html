FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Key information before ordering.

Click a question to expand the answer. For a specific project, contact us by email.

Can every table be made extendable?+

Most models can be made in either a fixed or extendable version. We select the mechanism to suit the tabletop shape, table dimensions and required extended length.

How long does production take?+

The standard lead time is approximately 2–3 weeks after all project details have been approved and materials ordered.

Can I choose any sintered stone finish?+

Yes. You can choose any sintered stone available from a manufacturer or distributor. If you have not selected a finish yet, we will help match the colour and surface to your interior.

Do you make tables to custom dimensions?+

Yes. Every table is made to order. You can choose the length, width, tabletop shape, base type, steel colour and a fixed or extendable version.

Do you deliver across Europe?+

Yes. We arrange secure delivery of the finished table to addresses across Europe.

How does the ordering process work?+

Send us approximate dimensions, an interior photo or an inspiration image. We then agree the materials and structure, prepare an individual quote and begin production after your approval.

How much does a table cost?+

The price depends on the dimensions, selected sintered stone, base design and whether the table is fixed or extendable. Prices start from €2,000 and every project is quoted individually.
