PROPORCJE

How much space do you need around the table?

Choosing the right dimensions is about more than the number of chairs. Clearance for pulling chairs out and moving through the room also matters. We help select a size that keeps the interior comfortable to use.

Send a floor plan, room dimensions or a simple photo showing the intended location. We will recommend a suitable size range.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions before ordering.

Every project is quoted individually. Simply send approximate dimensions, the preferred shape and an inspiration image.

Do you make non-standard dimensions?+

Yes. Every project is assessed individually, including dimensions outside common sizes such as 180 × 100 or 200 × 100 cm.

Can I send my own inspiration?+

Yes. A photo, sketch or visualisation helps define the desired style. We then develop a solution that is both buildable and structurally sound.

What table size works for eight people?+

A length of around 200–220 cm is usually suitable, although the final size depends on chair width, base design and the level of comfort you want.

Can a fixed table be converted to extendable later?+

The mechanism must be planned at the design stage, so the table should be specified as fixed or extendable from the outset.

Can I choose any leg colour?+

We offer a wide range of finishes, including black, white, anthracite, gold and many RAL colours.

How much does a made-to-measure table cost?+

The price depends mainly on the dimensions, selected sintered stone, base structure and extension mechanism. Every project is quoted individually.
