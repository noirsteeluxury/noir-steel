Why is sintered stone ideal for everyday dining?

Sintered stone combines the look of natural stone with a surface designed for everyday use. It is easy to clean, resists common stains and handles heat very well.

  • a wide range of finishes: marble, concrete, travertine and deep black
  • a surface suited to dining rooms and open-plan living spaces
  • resistance to UV light and everyday stains
  • a premium appearance without the sealing required by natural stone

FAQ

Frequently asked questions before ordering.

Every project is quoted individually. Simply send approximate dimensions, the preferred shape and an inspiration image.

Can sintered stone crack?+

Sintered stone is highly durable but remains a ceramic material. The edges are the most vulnerable, which is why the structure and tabletop support must be properly designed.

Can I place a hot pan on it?+

Sintered stone handles high temperatures well. We still recommend using trivets to protect the entire table and maintain good everyday habits.

Does sintered stone scratch?+

It is considerably more scratch-resistant than many typical furniture surfaces. A chopping board is still recommended to protect both knives and tabletop edges.

What tabletop thickness is best?+

Thickness is selected to suit the size, pattern and table structure. We can create either a visually light or more substantial appearance.

How do I clean a sintered stone tabletop?+

A soft cloth, warm water and a mild detergent are usually sufficient. Sintered stone does not require regular sealing.

Do you deliver across Europe?+

Yes. Tables are made to order and delivered across Europe.
